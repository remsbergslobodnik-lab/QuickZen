Your 60-Second Path to Quick Zen

Preparation: Set a 60-second timer. Find a comfortable spot‚Äîanywhere works. Read each step, close your eyes to practice, then proceed.

  1. Inhale Deeply. Breathe in through your nose for a count of four, filling your lungs with fresh energy.
  2. Ground Yourself. Close your eyes and feel the earth's support beneath you. Let gravity pull away tension.
  3. Observe Your Breath. Notice the gentle rise and fall of your chest without altering it.
  4. Box Breathing Cycle. Inhale for 4... Hold for 4... Exhale for 4... Hold for 4. Feel the rhythm.
  5. Visualize Calm. Imagine a serene green forest enveloping you, washing away worries.
  6. Extend the Exhale. Now, inhale 4... Hold 4... Exhale 6... Release fully.
  7. Repeat and Relax. One more cycle: In 4, hold 4, out 6. Sink deeper into peace.
  8. Return Gently. Let breathing normalize. Sense the quiet within.
  9. Awaken Mindfully. Wiggle fingers and toes, stretching lightly.
  10. Open Your Eyes. Emerge refreshed, carrying this serenity forward.
